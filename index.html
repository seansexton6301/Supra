Toyota Supra Mk4

The Iconic A80 Supra!!

The Toyota Supra Mk4 (A80), produced from 1993 to 2002, is a legendary sports car celebrated for its timeless design and incredible performance. Powered by the iconic 2JZ-GTE 3.0-liter twin-turbo inline-six engine, the Mk4 became a tuning legend and a cultural icon—famously featured in movies like The Fast and the Furious.

Key Features

  • ~320 horsepower (stock 2JZ-GTE, US spec)
  • 0-60 mph in ~4.6 seconds
  • Twin-turbocharged 2JZ-GTE engine
  • 6-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission

With its robust engineering and massive tuning potential! (the 2JZ is famous for handling 1000+ hp reliably), the Supra Mk4 remains a favorite among car enthusiasts and collectors worldwide.
